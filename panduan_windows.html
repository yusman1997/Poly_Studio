📺 How do I share my screen via HDMI?

You can share your laptop screen to the meeting room display using the HDMI cable connected to the Poly Studio X50.

What do I need to check first?

  • Ensure the Poly Studio X50 and TV are turned on.
  • Ensure the HDMI cable is available and connected to the HDMI port on the meeting room table.
  • Prepare your laptop for screen sharing.
💻 Laptop
↓
🔌 HDMI Cable
↓
📹 Poly Studio X50 System
↓
📺 TV / Display

Steps to Connect

🔌 Step 1

Connect your laptop to the available HDMI port on the meeting room table.
⏳ Step 2

Wait a few seconds for the system to detect your device.
📺 Step 3

Your laptop screen will automatically appear on the TV.
💻 Step 4

If the screen does not appear, follow the instructions below:
For Windows
  1. Press Windows + P
  2. Select:
  • ✅ Duplicate – Show the same screen on both displays
  • ✅ Extend – Use the TV as an additional display
For Mac
  1. Open System Settings
  2. Select Displays
  3. Enable Mirror Display
🔌 Step 5

Disconnect the HDMI cable after your presentation is finished.

Tips

  • Use a direct HDMI-to-HDMI cable connection.
  • Make sure the cable is connected properly.
  • Restart your laptop if the display is not detected.
